Scoring for Graham-Style Value Screening

Graham Criteria (simplified):

  • PE < 15 (trailing 12 months)
  • PB < 1.5 (or PE x PB < 22.5)
  • Dividend yield > 2/3 of AAA bond yield (~4%+ in current environment)
  • Current ratio > 2 (or debt ratio < 50%) * — adjusted for financials
  • 10+ years of consecutive profitability
  • Stable earnings growth (no >10% decline in last 2 years)
